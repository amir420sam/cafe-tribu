About Café Tribu

Bringing the Flavors of Persia, Turkey, and the Mediterranean to Your Plate

Our Chefs' Culinary Journey

At Café Tribu, we are passionate about sharing the rich culinary traditions of Persia, Turkey, and the broader Mediterranean region. Our skilled culinary team hails from the heart of the Middle East, bringing with them decades of experience in crafting authentic and innovative Persian, Turkish, and Mediterranean dishes. Led by Head Chef Arman Keshavarz and Sous Chef Aysun Özdemir, our chefs have trained in the finest kitchens across Iran, Turkey, and Greece, mastering the delicate balance of flavors that defines the regional cuisines.

Ingredients Sourced with Care

We take pride in sourcing the highest quality, locally-grown ingredients to ensure each dish that leaves our kitchen is a true reflection of seasonality and sustainability. From the saffron-infused rice dishes of Persia to the herbaceous salads of the Levant, every element on your plate has been thoughtfully selected to deliver an unparalleled sensory experience.

A Feast for the Senses

Step into Café Tribu and be transported to the bustling markets and intimate family gatherings that inspired our menu. The aroma of freshly baked pita, the sizzle of lamb kebabs on the grill, and the bright, bold flavors in every bite will leave you craving more. Join us in celebrating the culinary traditions that have nourished generations, one dish at a time.
